Administration Division

Administration Division is responsible for Personal Management and General Administration pertaining to the activities of the Commission. Staff Development for TVEC is the main function of this division and,

  • Establish policies and procedures to enable effective and efficient functions of the Commission
  • Ensure the condition and safe keeping of physical assets and vehicles etc
  • Recruitment of staff required by the Commission on the approval of Management Services Department of the Treasury
